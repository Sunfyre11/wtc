About Us

Our story, mission & the women behind WTC

Our Founding Story

Born from a Shared Passion for Exploration

It all started on 20th January 2019 — a group of Facebook friends with a common interest got together and went on a tour. Nobody thought this small trip would grow into something so significant.

One trip after another, and with a love for writing travelogues, more friends with a shared passion for travel and exploration joined the journey. That's how the Facebook group "Women's Travel Club Kolkata" was born in April 2019, managed by Jhumur Nandy.

"We don't plan for common travel destinations — we discover new places and execute them professionally so members can have a hassle-free, memorable tour every time."

— Jhumur Nandy, Founder & Admin

Mission & Vision

🌏

Our Mission

To enable solo women travellers to chase their dreams and passion for travel — and to build a community for all who love adventure and want to experience the vivid cultural diversity of our country and beyond.

🛡️

Our Vision

To create and maintain a group that puts utmost effort into ensuring safety and security for all women travellers, so they can enjoy and travel to new, beautiful, and unexplored places they have always dreamed of.

🗺️

Offbeat First

We specialize in destinations that most travel groups overlook. From hidden valleys of Himachal to remote Odisha sanctuaries — WTC takes you where others don't.

👭

Inclusive Community

From college students to senior citizens — our trips welcome women of all ages, backgrounds, and fitness levels. Every member's comfort and joy matters equally.
