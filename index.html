About Women's Travel Club

Where Solo Travellers
Find Their Tribe

It all started on 20th January, 2019, when a group of Facebook friends with a shared passion for travel set out together. What began as one trip grew into a thriving community of women explorers, managed by Jhumur Nandy.

We specialize in offbeat, uncommon destinations — places that most travel groups don't go. Every trip is planned with safety, comfort, and unforgettable experiences at its core.
